This is a very delicious burger and I will teach you how to make it yourself

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • mayo

Directions

  1. First get ingredients
  2. Put the meat on the bottom bun
  3. Put the cheese on top of the meat
  4. Add any other topping on top
  5. Place top bun on top of everything
  6. Enjoy!!
